Cantaloupe Smoothie

Description

This is a very simple recipe! A sweet, healthy way to start your day with a delicious, unique flavor.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup vanilla yogurt
  • ½ cup orange juice
  • ½ cup sliced cantaloupe
  • 4 ice cubes, crushed, or as needed

Steps

  1. Blend yogurt, orange juice, cantaloupe, and ice cubes in a blender until smooth, about 30 seconds.
